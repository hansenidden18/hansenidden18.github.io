I am a second year CS PhD Student at <a href="https://cs.vt.edu/">Virginia Tech</a>. I received a B.Informatics degree from <a href="https://www.its.ac.id/">Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS)</a> with cum laude distinction. My unmatched perspicacity coupled with sheer indefatigability makes me love research with high curiosity.

I have broad interests in **Storage Systems**, **Memory Systems**, and **Systems Architecture**.
